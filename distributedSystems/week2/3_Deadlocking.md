## Dealing with Deadlock
* `Prevention`: Ensuring that the 4 causes of deadlock cannot occur in the first place.
* `Avoid`: Even though it is possible for all 4 causes to occur at the same time, ensure that it never happens.
* `Detect`: Detect that a deadlock has occurred and resolve it.
